Welcome

Introduction

I live in the Silicon Valley of the Northwest. I love computers, particulary using different kinds of software. I first encountered computers in fifth grade and never looked back. One of my greatest joys is playing with new interfaces and tools.

Work and Education History

I am a nontraditional student on the IT track. Unable to continute going to college back in the 1990s, I took jobs in retail and housekeeping, while continuing non-official computer study and practice. In some of those jobs I wound up being "unofficial" IT for low-key items, since asking the janitor was easier than picking up the phone or submitting an online ticket. I also volunteer on The Darwin Awards site as a moderator, going on thirteen years now.

For Fun

I enjoy reading all the things; my only criteria for reading something is "does it look interesting." I like writing my own stories, usually a combination of soft science fiction and harder fantasy. I also enjoy video games, although which ones I play is restricted by poor reaction times and reflexes. I interact with people online in various communities, building associations and friendships.
